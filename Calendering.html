CELLFAB OneNote

After drying, the electrode undergoes a compaction process called calendaring.

The copper or aluminium foil coated on both sides is compressed by a rotating pair of rollers which generates a precisely defined calendaring pressure.

• The button "CELLFAB OneNote" directs you to the OneNote Notebook of the process for more information.

• Start the calendering process by pressing the "Start Calendering" button at bottom left.

Please enter the value for each variables of the calendering process. Then, press 'Write to InfluxDB' button
